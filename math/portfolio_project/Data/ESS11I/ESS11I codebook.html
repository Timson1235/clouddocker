Variables

  • name - Title of dataset
  • essround - ESS round
  • edition - Edition
  • proddate - Production date
  • cntry - Country
  • idno - Respondent's identification number
  • intagea - Age of interviewer
  • intgndr - Gender of interviewer
  • vdcond - Interview conducted, how
  • resclq - Clarification of questions
  • resrelq - Respondent reluctant to answer
  • resbab - Respondent tried to answer questions to the best of ability
  • resundq - Respondent understood the questions
  • preintf - Anyone present who interfered with the interview
  • prewhp - Husband/wife/partner interfered with interview
  • presd - Son/daughter (inc. Step/adopted) interfered with interview
  • preppil - Parent/parent in law interfered with interview
  • preorel - Other relative interfered with interview
  • prenrel - Other non-relative interfered with interview
  • predk - Don't know who interfered with interview
  • prenap - Who interfered with interview not applicable
  • prena - Who interfered with interview no answer
  • intlnga - Interview questionnaire language
  • intnum - Interviewer's identification number
